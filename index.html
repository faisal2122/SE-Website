About Department

The BS in SE degree equips students with theoretical as well as technical knowledge of the fundamentals of software engineering, algorithm analysis, data structures, programming languages, web development, and mobile application development.

There are courses in Software Quality Assurance to ensure software correctness, reliability, and safety, with the goal of producing high-quality software.

Courses such as Software Requirement Engineering, Software Design and Architecture, Software Construction and Development, Formal Methods in Software Engineering, Empirical Software Engineering, and Software Reverse Engineering provide students with the theoretical foundation and technical knowledge of software development.

About IUB

The Islamia University of Bahawalpur (IUB) is a public research university located in Bahawalpur, Pakistan. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher learning in Pakistan. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines.

Established in 1975, IUB is committed to providing quality education, fostering research, and promoting community engagement. The university has a diverse and vibrant campus environment that encourages intellectual growth, critical thinking, and innovation.
